ABOUT

____________________________________

 

DEBACLE 3      is a conceptual game without fancy graphics.

 

Press alt + <enter> key combination to maximize or minimize the window. Game controls are good when window is maximized.

 

YOU CAN SHIFT GEARS ANYTIME DURING GAME TO INCREASE OR DECREASE THE SPEED.

 

By default, 3 game tracks have been provided.

 

 You can also make your own game tracks.

 

 NOTE: When asked to PRESS ANY KEY… 

              please don't press arrow keys. Press any

               other key.

 

HOW TO MAKE YOUR OWN TRACKS

____________________________________

 

The width of the game window is of

 

 80 characters.

 

 And it reads track from a simple notepad file with 80 characters in each line (including blank spaces).

 

The vehicle is allowed to move only on blank spaces (marked by spacebar).

 

So simply make any sort of track you want.

 

(I don't have to tell you that you can use simple copy paste and replace features of notepad instead of typing every single character)

 

 

Use following symbols for colors of obstructions:

 

+       GREEN

 

/        BROWN

 

*       RED

 

-        BLUE     (causes most damage to vehicle)

 

IMPORTANT:

DO NOT USE LESS THAN OR MORE THAN

80 CHARACTERS IN A SINGLE LINE.

 

THE FILENAME OF TRACK CAN BE OF 8 CHARACTERS AT MAXIMUM.

 

Also, e.g. IF YOU SAVE YOUR

TRACK (i.e. NOTEPAD FILE) AS GALAXY, THEN AFTER OPTION 4 OF

THE MENU, ENTER FILENAME AS GALAXY.TXT

 

MAKE SURE THAT FONT TYPE IN NOTEPAD IS COURIER OR COURIER NEW BECAUSE OTHERWISE CHARACTERS WON'T ALIGN EXACTLY BELOW EACH OTHER AND IT WILL BE DIFFICULT TO MAKE TRACKS.

 

see provided tracks:

 

 R1.TXT,

 R2.TXT,

 R3.TXT                 for example.

 

BUT IT IS RECOMMENDED THAT BEFORE THIS YOU SHOULD

PLAY THEM FIRST SO THAT YOU DON'T STEAL AWAY FUN OF PLAYING THEM FROM YOURSELF.

 

ABOUT COMPUTER GENERATED RANDOM TRACKS

____________________________________

 

More simply, let computer create the tracks for you by choosing option 5 of the menu.

 

Since it simply puts random chunks anywhere, the track generated may be easy or difficult.

 

BEWARE OF BLUE OBSTRUCTIONS. THEY CAUSE SEVERE DAMAGE TO YOUR VEHICLE.
